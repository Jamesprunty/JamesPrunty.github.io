The three letter game

Three Letter Game Rules



Background

I thought of this game when on the road. Most of the UK license plates end in three letters so I started trying to create words out of them. Hope you enjoy this variation.

Premise

You will be given 5 sets of 3 letters. You must use the letters to make a word / phrase. The points are scored on letter count and spaces will cost 5 extra points so try to keep it to one word if possible.

The Rules



1 - Each word must be more than 2 letters long.
2 - The total characters must be more than 3 letters (If the prompt is "ear" you could not input "ear", you would have to input "bear").
3 - The letters must come in order (they can be repeated). (eg, if your letters are "iec", "rice" would not work but "piece" would)

Hope you have fun!
