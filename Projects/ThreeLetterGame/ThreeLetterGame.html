The three letter game

The game is simple, You will be shown 3 letters, you have to come up with the shortest word possible that contain those letters in that order.
You can use multiple words, but, additional words will cost you 3 more points.
Each letter added to the 3 letters will add one point.
you want the least amount of points, with the most amount of words.
You score at the end will be calculated based on the amount of words you did and how many points each cost.
